The Digital World is Under Attack

Every day, thousands of people in Singapore fall victim to scams, phishing attacks, and identity theft. In 2023 alone, Singaporeans lost over $660 million to scams — and the number keeps rising.

🎯

You Are the Target

Cybercriminals don't just target banks and corporations. They target you — through your phone, your email, your social media accounts. Anyone online is a potential victim. Knowledge is your most powerful weapon.
